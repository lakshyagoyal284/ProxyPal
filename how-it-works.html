The Full Workflow

Four simple steps to reclaim your day

1
Post Your Task
Tell us what you need

Describe the task — whether it's standing in a queue at the passport office, waiting for a delivery at home, or submitting forms at a government department. Tell us the location, preferred date & time, duration, and your budget. It takes less than 2 minutes to post.

2
Get Matched
A ProxyPal accepts your task

Nearby verified ProxyPals see your task and can accept it within minutes. Each ProxyPal has an ID-verified profile with ratings and reviews from previous clients. You can see who's available and pick the best match, or let the system match you automatically.

3
Track Progress
Live updates in real time

Once your ProxyPal starts working, you get live updates — check-in, progress milestones, photos, and real-time chat. You can see exactly where they are in the queue, how much longer it will take, and communicate directly if anything changes.

4
Pay & Rate
Release payment & review

After the task is completed, review the work and release payment. Your payment is held securely until you're satisfied. Then rate your ProxyPal and leave a review to help the community. Earn rewards for frequent usage and referrals!
